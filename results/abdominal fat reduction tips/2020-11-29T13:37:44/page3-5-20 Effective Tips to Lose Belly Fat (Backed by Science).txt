20 Effective Tips to Lose Belly Fat (Backed by Science)

Eat plenty of soluble fiber. Soluble fiber absorbs water and forms a gel that helps slow down food as it passes through your digestive system.
Avoid foods that contain trans fats. Trans fats are created by pumping hydrogen into unsaturated fats, such as soybean oil.
Don’t drink too much alcohol. Alcohol can have health benefits in small amounts, but it’s seriously harmful if you drink too much.
Eat a high protein diet. Protein is an extremely important nutrient for weight management.
