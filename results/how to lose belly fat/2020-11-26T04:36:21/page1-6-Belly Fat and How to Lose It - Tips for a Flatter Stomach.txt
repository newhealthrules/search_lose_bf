There are four keys to controlling belly fat: exercise, diet, sleep, and stress management. 1. Exercise: Vigorous exercise trims all your fat, including visceral fat. 2. Diet: There is no magic diet for belly fat. 3. Sleep: Getting the right amount of shut-eye helps. 4. Stress: Everyone has stress.
