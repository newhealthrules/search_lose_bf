How to lose belly fat?
Opt for for cardiovascular exercises. Losing weight and losing belly fat go hand in hand, and neither can be accomplished without exercise. If you’re looking for instant weight loss, engage in cardiovascular exercises. Studies reveal that cardio is one of the most effective forms of exercises to lose weight around your waist.
